Chicken Shawarma Bowl

Delectable Chicken Bowl

Ingredients List (serves 4)

Marinade
  • 1/2 c yogurt
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Mix together marinade in a bowl until combined. Slice 4 chicken breasts in strips, add to marinade. Let marinate minimum 2 hrs, optimal overnight.

Shawarma Dressing (can be doubled)
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 4-5 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 c mayo
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric
  • 1/2 tsp coriander
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
Stir all ingredient from dressing list until well combined. Preheat oven 350 F place marinated chicken on foil lined cookie sheet. Cook until internal temp 160F is reached. While chicken is cooking prepare salad.

Salad Ingredients

  • 2 heads romaine lettuce, washed chopped
  • 2 tomatoes diced
  • 1 cucumber chopped bite size pieces
  • 1 red onion diced
  • 1.5 cups sliced peppers (red,green,yellow or all)
  • optional 2 c cooked rice (sounds odd, but is quite good mixed with lettuce)

Instructions

  1. In 4 bowls add 1/4 c rice (if used) in each bowl
  2. Add preferred amount of salad ingredients, and sliced marinated chicken strips
  3. Drizzle with Shawarma dressing, and stir all ingredients together
