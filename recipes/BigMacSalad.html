Big Mac Salad

Healthy delicious alternative

Ingredients serves four

Dressing
  • 3/4 c mayo
  • 4 tsp mustard
  • 2 tbsp dill pickples chopped small
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp minced onion
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
    • Salad
  • 1 lb ground beef (fancy alternative 1lb sliced steak)
  • 2 romaine heads washed and chopped
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 1/4 c sliced dill pickles
  • 1 c old fort cheese shredded

Instructions

  1. Mix mayo, mustard, dill pickles, vinegar, chopped onion, honey and paprika in a bowl until thoroughly combined. Refridgerate until ready to assemble salad.
  2. Heat 10 in saute pan over medium head. Add ground beef, cook until browned and no longer pink. Drain off fat
  3. If using steak, cook as per your liking, I prefer medium well done. Slice thinly
  4. Wash and chop lettuce. Slice onion in half and thinly slice. Rough chop dill pickles.
  5. When ready to serve, toss lettuce, onions, pickles and cheese
  6. Put salad mixture evenly into 4 bowls, place 1/4 of ground beef mixture into each bowl. If using steak divide steak evenly between 4 bowls. Drizzle with dressing and serve immediately.
